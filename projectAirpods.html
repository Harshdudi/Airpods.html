Troubleshooting

Device won't turn on

Ensure the case is fully charged. Leave the earbuds in the case plugged into a power source for 30 minutes.

Left earbud has no sound

Place both earbuds in the case, close the lid for 10 seconds, then reopen. If the issue persists, perform a factory reset.

Error Code: Red Flashing Light

This indicates a hardware pairing error. Press and hold the back button for 15 seconds to hard-reset.
